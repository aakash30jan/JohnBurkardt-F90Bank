TRIANGLE_WANDZURA_RULE
High Order Quadrature in a Triangle.

TRIANGLE_WANDZURA_RULE is a FORTRAN90 library which can return any of six Wandzura rules for high order quadrature over the interior of a triangle in 2D.

There are six rules, which have polynomial degree of exactness of 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30.
