ROT13
ROT13 Character Cipher

ROT13 is a FORTRAN90 library which is given a string and applies the ROT13 cipher to characters and the ROT5 cipher to digits.

For example, we have: 

        Input:                      Output:
 
        abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz  nopqrstuvwxyzabcdefghijklm
        Cher                        Pure
        James Thurston Howell       Wnzrf Guhefgba Ubjryy
        0123456789                  56789012345
