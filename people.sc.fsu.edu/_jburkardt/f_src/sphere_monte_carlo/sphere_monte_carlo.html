SPHERE_MONTE_CARLO
Monte Carlo Estimate of Sphere Surface Integrals

SPHERE_MONTE_CARLO is a FORTRAN90 library which estimates the integral of F(X,Y,Z) over the surface of the unit sphere in 3D.

Languages:

SPHERE_MONTE_CARLO is available in a C version and a C++ version and a FORTRAN77 version and a FORTRAN90 version and a MATLAB version and a Python version.
