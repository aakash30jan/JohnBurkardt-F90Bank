LINE_MONTE_CARLO
Monte Carlo Estimate of Integrals Along Unit Line in 1D.

LINE_MONTE_CARLO is a FORTRAN90 library which uses the Monte Carlo method to estimate the integral of a function F(X) over the length of the unit line in 1D.
