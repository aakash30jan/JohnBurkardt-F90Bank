TRIANGLE_ANALYZE
Compute Properties of a Triangle

TRIANGLE_ANALYZE is a FORTRAN90 program which reads a triangle defined in a file, and uses the TRIANGLE_PROPERTIES library to compute angles, area, centroid, circumcircle, edge lengths, incircle, orientation, orthocenter, and quality.

Usage:

triangle_analyze filename
where filename contains the vertex coordinates.
