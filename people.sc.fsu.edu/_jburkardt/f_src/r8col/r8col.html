R8COL
Arrays of Columns

R8COL is a FORTRAN90 library which contains utility routines for R8COL's, that is, double precision real MxN arrays, considered as N column vectors, each of length M. The data may be thought of as a matrix of multiple columns, and many operations will be carried out columnwise.
